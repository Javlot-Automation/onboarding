Important – Initial Deposit Instructions

To activate your access on the broker side, a first deposit of exactly €1,000 is required.

Please note that there is no benefit in depositing more than €1,000 at this stage. On the contrary, we strongly recommend not exceeding this amount for now.

Here is why:

The next step of the onboarding process (Step 7) includes a mandatory verification trade required by the broker as part of their account validation procedure. This trade must be placed with a lot size of 1, which is unusually high for an initial setup.

Because of this, depositing more than €1,000 at this stage would only increase unnecessary exposure, without providing any advantage.

Rest assured:

  • This verification step is standard and temporary
  • We will guide you step by step through the process
  • You will receive clear best practices to execute this trade safely
  • There is no need to stress – the goal is validation, not performance

Once this step is completed, your account will be fully validated and ready to operate under normal conditions.

Once on your Bullwaves dashboard, click on the 'Fund Account' section in the left-hand panel. You will then be taken to the window displayed below. Make sure to select the correct account and enter 1,000€ (or equivalent) in the amount field.

On the right side, choose your preferred payment method and click the 'Deposit Funds' button. From there, simply complete the deposit process.

To fund your account, go to the 'Funds' tab in the left-hand menu. You will then arrive on the page displayed below. Make sure to select your newly created account and enter 1,000 in the amount field. Then, simply click the 'Continue' button.

From here, choose your preferred payment method to continue funding your account.

On your Axi Dashboard, click on the "Fund" section on the left hand side, to arrive on the page shown below. From here select your funding method and enter your details.

Once you reach the page shown in the image below, make sure to enter €1,000 (or equivalent) in the appropriate field.

On your Startrader dashboard, click on the ‘Fund’ section on the left-hand side to reach the page shown below. From there, make sure to select your newly created account and enter a funding amount of at least €1,000 (or equivalent).

From this page, select your preferred payment method to proceed with account funding.

On your VT Markets dashboard, click on the ‘Funds’ > ‘Deposit’ section on the left-hand side to reach the page shown below. From there, make sure to select your newly created account and enter a funding amount of at least €1,000 (or equivalent).

From this page, select your preferred payment method to proceed with account funding.

On the Vantage Markets dashboard, use the ‘Deposit’ button on your newly created MetaTrader 5 account to fund it.

On reaching this windows, make sure to verify you selected your newly created account (normaly automatic). And introduce your desired fund amount (at least 1000€ or equivalent in for your currency).

From here, choose your preferred payment method to continue funding your account.
