MetaTrader Setup

Need Help?

Locate your MT5 credentials, log in to MetaTrader 5, and prepare your trading pairs.

Now that you have created and funded your MetaTrader 5 account, you will need your login credentials. These are always sent to your email inbox by your selected broker.

To help you find them, you can search for 'MT5 Account' in your mailbox. Also make sure to check your spam folder. The email should look similar to the example shown below.

Note: Bullwaves users: your MT5 account password is the same as your Bullwaves account password.

You can now open MetaTrader 5 on your phone and begin the login process. When the app opens, you may be prompted to 'Open a demo account'. Simply close this prompt. You will then need to log in to your 'Trade Account'.

Next, enter your server name in the search bar and select the first result that matches your broker's name. Finally, enter your MetaTrader 5 account credentials (sent to you by email), and you will be logged in.

Once you are logged in, it's time to prepare your account for trading. You will need to add two quotes, which depend on your broker. Each broker uses its own specific pair of quotes. Please refer to the table below and add the two quotes corresponding to your selected broker.

Your trading account is fully setup and ready for the anti-bot validation procedure.
