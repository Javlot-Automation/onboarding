Connect to MT5 App

Need Help?

Link your broker account to the MetaTrader 5 app on your phone.

1
Open the MetaTrader 5 app and go to Settings > New Account.
2
Search for your broker's name: Vantage
3
Select the server name provided in your broker's email.
4
Enter your Login (Account Number) and Password.
You should have received these details via email from your broker after creating your account. Please check your inbox (and spam folder).
