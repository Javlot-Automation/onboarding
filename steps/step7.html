Verification Trade

Need Help?

Open a 0.01 lot trade on EURUSD to verify your account connection.

1
Go to the Quotes tab in your MT5 app
2
Find EURUSD (or add it if missing)
3
Tap on it and select Trade
4
Verify Lot Size is 0.01
5
Tap Buy by Market
6
Go to Trade tab, tap the position > Close Position
7
Tap Close by Market
Market Hours Notice
The Forex market is closed on weekends.
It opens: Sunday 5PM EST (New York) / Monday 00:00 (platform time).
If the market is closed, you can skip this step and continue setup.
