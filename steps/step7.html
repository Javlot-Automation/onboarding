Set up your verification trade in MetaTrader 5.

You will now need to set up the account verification trade.
Go to the 'Trade' section in your MetaTrader 5 application and tap the '+' button. You will then be able to modify your trade settings.

Set the trading pair to EURUSD, corresponding to the quote you added in the previous step (refer to the image to confirm the EURUSD quote linked to your broker).
Make sure to enter a lot size of 1.00. This is mandatory, and the verification will fail if a different lot size is used.

Before proceeding with any action, please read this entire step carefully.

This step must be handled with extreme caution in order to protect your trading capital as much as possible.
When you are ready, click the 'Buy by Market' button. Your trade will start immediately.
Wait for at least 15 seconds, then close the trade.

That's it. Congratulations! Your account is now fully validated and ready to be connected to Javlot.
