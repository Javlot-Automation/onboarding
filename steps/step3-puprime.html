Need Help?

Enter your personal details, configure your MT5 account, then complete your KYC verification.

Once your PU Prime account is created, you will need to enter your personal details. After clicking “Next”, you will be prompted to set up your trading account.

Choose Trading Platform: Recommended – MetaTrader 5
Choose Account Type: Standard
Choose An Account Currency: EUR is recommended.

By ticking the box: make sure to read the user compliance before creating your account.

Next: once you are ready and have double checked your selections, click on "Next" to create your MT5 account.

You will now be asked to complete the KYC (Know Your Customer) process. Once it’s done, you should arrive on the page shown below.
